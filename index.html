PROJECTS

Here is a selection of my most recent projects.

TURN-BASED TACTICS

Turn-Based Tactics is a prototype of an Event Based System that handle Turn Management between Player and AI. In order to battle your way thru two unique encounters, the player has to stategicaly gear up his party, choose actions thru the usage of a custom built UI System, cast spell of various types and defeat his opponents.

Learn more

DUCK HUNTER VR

DuckHunter is a VR prototype using the new XR Input System of Unity. Play thru our various game mode this classic remake and reach the highest score on the board. Additional feature is available for more mature public enabling player to explode ducks on the go as well as a variety of weapons for all the gun freaks out there.

Learn more

WAVE-CLEAR RPG

Wave-ClearRPG is a prototype of a Wave System showcasing thru multiple level the capability two different AI System. Fight along our protagonist and clear waves keeping in mind your sole objective is to survive.

WOLFENSTEIN 3D REMAKE

Set foot in the universe of the classic game Wolfenstein 3D with our own Remake created with openGL and SDL in C++. Fight Germain AI, manage your bullets and be safe.

PIGGYBREAKER

Welcome to Piggybreaker! A bubble breaker game made with SDL in C++ setting its theme on a farm. Fire up this cannon and break some bubble has you test out our Collision System and increase your highest score.
